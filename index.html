Model story

Why PU after baseline

  1. Baseline: proves the card-level behavior separates known business cards from consumer background.
  2. Problem: consumer cards are not clean negatives, they may include hidden business users.
  3. PU ranking: treats business as positive and consumer as unlabeled, then ranks business-likeness.
  4. Action: converts ranking into review, campaign and watchlist queues.

Metrics

What the numbers mean

The near perfect supervised metrics show that known business and consumer segments are easy to separate in this synthetic dataset. They do not prove that every top consumer lead is truly an entrepreneur.

PU metrics are proxy validation: they check if known business cards are ranked above consumer background.

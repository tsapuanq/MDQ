Model approach

Baseline first, PU for the final ranking

Baseline proves the feature signal. PU is the final framing because consumer cards are unlabeled background, not clean negatives.

01 Supervised baseline

Known business cards are compared with consumer cards to validate that features separate business-like behavior.

02 Baseline sanity checks

Supervised probability is compared with anomaly score and distance to the business centroid.

03 PU learning

Business cards are positives. Consumer cards stay unlabeled. The output is a relative business-like rank.

04 Action tiers

The rank is converted into review, campaign, watchlist and no-action queues.
